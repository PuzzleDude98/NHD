Joseph Weizenbaum Bio

Joseph Weizenbaum was born on Jan. 8, 1923 in Berlin, Germany. He had a rough childhood. He had to flee from Nazi Germany when he was just 13, and gained depression from his father convincing him he was worthless.

He came to the US in 1935, and studied math at Wayne University in Detroit. Eventually he left to join US army air corps, and then he started work with Grand Electric in 1955. He finally game to MIT in 1962, and became a full professor in 1970.
